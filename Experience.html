Social Media

During the Summer of 2022, I worked as a Social Media Manager for an arts nonprofit called ArtSphereInc. It was here that I received training both on and off site by helping with day-to-day activities and also taking virtual training seminars.

As I said in the job title, I was the social media manager. It was my duty to gather stories from our employees and partners to write and upload to the company's social platforms. Thanks to the training seminars, I was able to boost the company's reach and message through the power of S.E.O. (Search Engine Optimization!)

Sporting Events

The Summer before last (2021), I worked with a sporting producer called "Celebrity Boxing LLC." I served as the Assistant to the General Counsel and it was my duty to make sure the stages were set, everyone was where they needed to be, and that we were clear filming.

During this job, I even got to meet the late Aaron Carter as he was the headline fight against Lamar Odom. Sadly, he did not win the fight that night, but it was really a great match to watch from my staff position.
